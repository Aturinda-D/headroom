# Headroom

> Your Financial Breathing Space

A mobile-first budgeting app that helps you spend confidently and live debt-free by showing exactly how much money you have available for guilt-free spending.

## What is Headroom?

Headroom is a personal budget assistant built around three core concepts:

1. **Projects** - Separate financial contexts (Personal, Business, Family Pool) that keep your finances organized
2. **Wallets** - Track actual money stores (Cash, Mobile Money, Bank Account) with real-time balances
3. **Categories** - Your primary navigation and spending organization (Food, Transport, Bills, etc.)

At the heart of it all is your **"Free to Spend"** amountâ€”a single number showing exactly what you can spend guilt-free after accounting for all your commitments.

The app works offline-first, so you can log expenses anywhere, anytime. Everything syncs seamlessly across your devices when you're back online.

## Key Features

- **Projects & Wallets** - Organize finances into separate contexts (Personal, Business, Family) with real money stores (Cash, Mobile Money, Bank)
- **Category-First Navigation** - Browse spending by category, each with its own budget and transaction history
- **Free to Spend Dashboard** - See your available spending money instantly (daily, weekly, or monthly tracking with automatic rollover)
- **Smart Recurring Transactions** - Set up bills and income once, let them auto-generate with optional confirmations
- **Offline-First** - Log transactions without internet, sync automatically later
- **Budget Goals & Alerts** - Set project-level and category-specific limits with intelligent notifications
- **Debt Tracking** - Monitor loan repayments and visualize your path to being debt-free
- **Cross-Device Sync** - Access your budget from phone, tablet, or web
